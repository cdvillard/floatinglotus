My name is Michelle DeBaun. I am a licensed massage therapist in the Miami area. I specialize in manual lymphatic drainage, tension headaches, and joint mobilization.

Services

Manual Lymphatic Drainage

Manual lymphatic drainage is a very gentle type of massage that helps your body excess fluid from between your cells. It promotes healing after surgery or trauma and aids in optimal immune function.

Joint Mobilization

Sometimes your muscles can become so tense and unbalanced that your joints' natural range of motion can be inhibited. Massage and stretching may be able to increase or even normalize your body's ability to move.

Thai Massage

Thai massage is also referred to Thai yoga or "lazy man's yoga." It is a combination of stretching and energy work. The client is positioned into yoga-like poses and massaged along energy lines. Thai massage can performed in 60-, 90-, or 120-minute sessions.

Swedish Massage

One of the most common modalities, Swedish massage is a full-body technique that loosens tight muscles and increases blood flow. It uses lotions and oils in combination with kneading and gliding strokes for general pain relief and relaxation.

Seated/Corporate Massage

Seated massage offers many of the benefits of massage using kneading and compression techniques without lubricant. This is an ideal modality when providing massage to groups at events or offices.
